How to Play

• Adjust the numbers in front of each compound to balance the equation

• The same number of each type of atom must appear on both sides of the equation

• Click "Check Balance" to verify your solution

• Use "Auto Solve" if you need help (reduces points earned)

• Complete puzzles to earn points before time runs out!
