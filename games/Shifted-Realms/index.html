How to Play

Objective

Rearrange the numbered tiles in ascending order with the empty space in the bottom-right corner.

Gameplay

  • Tap on any tile adjacent to the empty space to move it
  • On desktop, use arrow keys to move tiles
  • Arrange all tiles in numerical order to win

Controls

  • Shuffle: Randomize the board
  • Reset: Return to the initial shuffled state
  • Hint: Highlights a tile that's out of place
  • Swap: Swap two tiles (limited uses)
  • Undo: Revert your last move

Levels

  • 3x3: 8 tiles (Beginner)
  • 4x4: 15 tiles (Advanced)
  • 5x5: 24 tiles (Expert)
