How to Play Pulley System Puzzle

Your goal is to arrange pulleys to lift the weight with minimal effort.

  • Drag and drop pulleys from the inventory to the mounting points.
  • Connect pulleys by dragging the rope from one pulley to another.
  • Each pulley system has a mechanical advantage that reduces the force needed.
  • Fixed pulleys change the direction of force.
  • Movable pulleys provide a mechanical advantage of 2.
  • Compound systems multiply the mechanical advantage.

Objective: Create a pulley system that requires exactly 25N of force to lift the 100N weight (mechanical advantage of 4).

You win when your system matches the required mechanical advantage!
