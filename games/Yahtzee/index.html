How to Play Yahtzee

Game Instructions

  1. Click "Roll Dice" to start your turn. You can roll up to 3 times per turn.
  2. After each roll, you can click on dice to hold them (they won't be re-rolled).
  3. After your final roll, click "Score Roll" and choose a category to score.
  4. Each category can only be used once per game.
  5. The game consists of 13 turns, one for each category.
  6. Try to maximize your score by strategically choosing categories!

Scoring Rules

  • Upper Section: Score the sum of specific numbers (Ones, Twos, etc.)
  • Bonus: Get 35 bonus points if your upper section total is 63 or more
  • Three of a Kind: At least 3 same dice - score total of all dice
  • Four of a Kind: At least 4 same dice - score total of all dice
  • Full House: 3 of one number + 2 of another = 25 points
  • Small Straight: Sequence of 4 numbers = 30 points
  • Large Straight: Sequence of 5 numbers = 40 points
  • Yahtzee: All 5 dice the same = 50 points
  • Chance: Anything - score total of all dice
