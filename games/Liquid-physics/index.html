How to Play

Welcome to Liquid Physics Puzzle! Your goal is to complete the electrical circuit by filling the container with liquid.

Game Mechanics

  • Aim & Shoot: Touch and drag to aim the cannon, then release to shoot liquid
  • Fill Container: As liquid fills the container, it will lift floating objects
  • Complete Circuit: When liquid reaches both electrodes, it completes the circuit
  • Power the Light: Complete the circuit to power the lightbulb and win the level

Controls

  • Touch & Drag: Aim the cannon
  • Release: Shoot liquid
  • Reset Button: Restart current level
  • Next Button: Advance to next level (when available)
